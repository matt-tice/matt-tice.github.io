About Me

I recently (2025) completed my PhD in mathematics at the University of Regina under the supervision of Martin Frankland. My research is motivated by problems in mathematical physics, but often segues into problems in pure mathematics. In that vein, I'm particularly interested in problems in functional analysis, category theory, and Lie theory.

My undergraduate and masters degrees were both in physics. I try to use that background to flavour my current work, focusing on topics that are of interest to practicing physicists.
